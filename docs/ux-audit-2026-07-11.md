# UX/UI audit — Ecole (Layaida), 2026-07-11

Full-app review against: trilingual/RTL correctness, learner flows, Nord Campus
consistency, the interaction-craft doctrine, WCAG basics, responsive behavior,
and loading/empty/error states. Method: code review of all 12 screens +
components, grep predicates from CLAUDE.md, compiled-CSS verification.
Findings ranked by severity; each names the file and the concrete fix.
**Fixed same-day** items were committed with this audit (`2052f07`, `01aeab7`);
the rest are queued as ROADMAP tasks P1-T13…P1-T17.

## Executive summary

The Nord Campus implementation is in strong shape: tokens-only styling (one
compliant `themeColor` metadata hex aside), logical-properties grep is clean,
44px touch targets and visible focus rings are systematic, all three locales
route through `i18n.ts` with `TranslationKeys` enforcement, empty states
suggest actions, and the motion foundation (P1-T10/T11) follows the doctrine's
hard floor. The real gaps were: (1) an RTL regression that made **every
progress bar fill from the wrong side in Arabic**, (2) a set of
**English-only accessibility strings** leaking past the i18n law, (3) two
**flow dead ends** (quizzes bypassed by lesson navigation; no course-completion
moment), and (4) hand-rolled overlays (modal/drawer/sheet) that look right but
lack focus management. Nothing found blocks the Phase 1 goal; the two HIGH
items below were fixed immediately.

## Findings

### HIGH

1. **RTL: all progress bars filled left-to-right in Arabic — FIXED**
   `src/components/Progress.tsx`, quiz header in
   `src/app/course/[courseId]/quiz/[chapterId]/page.tsx`.
   P1-T10 converted `width` animations to `transform: scaleX()` with a
   hardcoded `origin-left`. Width-based fills were direction-aware for free;
   scaleX is not, so the ProgressBar (lesson header, course card, fiche cours)
   and the quiz progress strip regressed for `dir="rtl"`. The design handoff
   says "progress fills from inline-start". Fix shipped: `rtl:origin-right`
   (compiled rule verified: `:where(:dir(rtl),[dir=rtl],[dir=rtl] *)`).

2. **Flow: chapter quizzes are unreachable from the lesson sequence, and the
   course has no ending** — QUEUED (P1-T13)
   `src/app/course/[courseId]/lesson/[lessonId]/page.tsx`.
   "Suivant" and the 5s auto-advance walk `getAllLessons()` — straight past
   every chapter quiz into the next chapter. A student following the primary
   flow never meets a quiz; it exists only as a sidebar row (behind a drawer
   toggle on mobile). And on the last lesson, the next-button slot renders an
   empty `<span/>`: finishing a course produces no acknowledgment, no CTA —
   the strongest moment in the product is a dead end. The quiz results screen
   similarly ends at Rejouer / Retour au cours with no "continue" path.

### MEDIUM

3. **i18n: English-only strings reaching users in fr/ar — FIXED**
   The skip-to-content link ("Skip to content", visible on focus, 8 pages),
   `Navbar` aria-labels ("Main navigation", "Language: …", "Select language",
   "Open/Close menu"), and the lesson note-delete button (labelled with
   *close*, not *delete*). All moved into `common.*` keys in the three
   locales; note-delete now says "Supprimer/Delete/حذف". The documents-tab
   downloaded checkmark also gained sr-only text (was `title` only).

4. **Overlays lack focus management** — QUEUED (P1-T14)
   `src/components/Modal.tsx`, lesson chapters drawer, catalog filter sheet.
   `Modal` moves focus in and handles Escape but has no focus trap, no focus
   restore on close, and no body scroll lock. The two bottom sheets
   (hand-rolled, `role="dialog" aria-modal`) have none of the four — Tab
   walks into the page behind the sheet; Escape does nothing. The language
   dropdown in `Navbar` doesn't close on outside click or Escape.

5. **Locked lesson/quiz rows are focusable dead links** — QUEUED (P1-T14)
   `src/app/course/[courseId]/page.tsx` accordion. Non-enrolled rows render
   `<Link href="#">` + `preventDefault`; keyboard/screen-reader users land on
   a link that announces as navigable and does nothing, and the `Lock` icon
   is `aria-hidden` with no textual "locked" equivalent. Render a `<span>`
   (or `aria-disabled` button) with sr-only locked text instead.

6. **Composite widgets have ARIA roles without keyboard patterns** — QUEUED
   (P1-T15) Lesson tabs (`role="tab"`, no arrow keys, no roving tabindex, no
   `tabpanel`), `Segmented` (same, and it's also used for non-tab things like
   the locale/theme switchers — radiogroup semantics fit better), quiz answers
   and onboarding grades/goals (`role="radio"` as independent tab stops, no
   arrow-key movement). Usable today because every option is a real button,
   but the announced semantics promise interactions that don't work — WCAG
   4.1.2 territory.

7. **RTL: mixed-script chapter numbers + off-center skip link + unmirrored
   quiz arrow — FIXED**
   `formatNumber(locale, n).padStart(2, "0")` produced "0١" (Latin zero,
   Arabic-Indic one) on the fiche cours; now pads with the locale's own zero.
   `.skip-to-content` centered via `inset-inline-start: 50%` +
   `translate(-50%)`, which double-shifts under RTL; now direction-neutral
   `left: 50%`. The quiz feedback "revoir la leçon" `ArrowUpRight` was the
   single directional icon in the app not mirroring under `dir="rtl"`.

### LOW

8. **Overlay/toast motion gaps** — QUEUED (P1-T16). Bottom sheets, Modal and
   toasts pop in with no transition (`--ease-drawer` exists but is unused);
   `ProgressRing` animates at 500ms, past the doctrine's 300ms ceiling
   (already noted in the P1-T10 log). Page/dropdown enters are P1-T12's
   scope — this task covers the overlays it doesn't.
9. **`text-faint` fails AA in both themes** — QUEUED (P1-T17). Pre-existing,
   flagged during P1-T7 (2.81–3.06:1 in light mode) and deliberately left.
   It's used for real text (lesson durations, day labels, counts), not just
   decoration.
10. **Onboarding subjects step**: deselecting all three subjects silently
    re-applies all three at finish. Harmless fallback, but the button could
    disable at zero selected. Not queued; fold into any future onboarding
    task.
11. **Route metadata is French-only** (accepted P1-T5 constraint — locale is
    client-side; revisit if/when locale moves to the URL or a cookie
    readable at SSR time).

## Clean checks (for the record)

- `grep -rn 'transition-all' src` — empty; no animated
  width/height/padding/margin/top/left anywhere (the audit's origin-left find
  was a transform-origin issue, not an animated-layout-property one).
- Logical-utilities grep (`ml-/mr-/pl-/pr-/text-left/right`) — clean.
- Raw hex in components — clean (design tokens only).
- `prefers-reduced-motion` kill-switch present and covers the celebration
  keyframes; skeletons/`loading.tsx` mirror their routes; empty states all
  carry a recovery action; dark-mode token pairs pass AA per the P1-T7
  audit (minus `text-faint`, see #9).
- Celebration moments (P1-T11) verified in code: lesson complete (both
  paths) + quiz ≥60%, ≤300ms, transform/opacity/stroke only, `aria-hidden`.
- Mobile: filter sheet with live-count CTA, chapters drawer, sticky enroll
  CTA with `pb-24` clearance, hamburger nav — all present per the design.
